⚙️ Pipeline

How It Works

A complete NLP pipeline from raw text to sentiment prediction.

01
🧹

Text Preprocessing

Raw text is cleaned — URLs, HTML, and special characters are stripped. Stopwords are removed while keeping negation words.

02
🔤

Lemmatisation

Words are reduced to their base form using NLTK's WordNet Lemmatizer, improving feature generalisation.

03
📊

TF-IDF Vectorisation

Text is converted into numerical feature vectors using TF-IDF with up to 10 000 unigram + bigram features.

04
🤖

Multinomial Naive Bayes

A Multinomial Naive Bayes classifier analyzes word frequencies and predicts sentiment probabilities efficiently.
